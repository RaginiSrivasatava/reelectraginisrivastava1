About Ragini

Ragini Srivastava is the first person of Indian origin to serve as Town Clerk of North Hempstead. A successful entrepreneur, advocate, and public servant, she brings dedication and vision to her role. Her efforts span from empowering survivors of domestic abuse to championing small businesses and hosting cultural events.

Key Initiatives

  • Mobile Clerk Office & Extended Hours
  • Business Seminars & Minority Enterprise Support
  • Community Wellness Events: Yoga & Mindfulness
  • Domestic Abuse Advocacy & Legal Aid Support
  • Support for Seniors and Youth Civic Engagement
  • Film & TV Production Support through Town Permits

Signature Events

  • Diwali Celebration
  • Women's Roll of Honor
  • Indian Independence Day
  • September 11 Remembrance
  • Annual Vow Renewal Ceremony
